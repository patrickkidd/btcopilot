## Error Patterns Addressed

1. **Under-extraction of People**: AI was returning empty people arrays due to overly conservative "SPARSE OUTPUT" guidance → Added explicit PERSON EXTRACTION RULES emphasizing to extract ALL named individuals

2. **Title/Honorific Stripping**: AI was extracting "Linda" instead of "Aunt Linda" → Added example showing correct preservation of "Aunt Linda", "Uncle Tom", "Dr Brezel"

3. **Possessive Name Patterns**: GT uses patterns like "User's Mother", "Marcus' Girlfriend" for unnamed relatives → Added guidance to use possessive patterns for distinct identification
