# Fable 5 Extraction Experiment — FINAL

**Date**: 2026-06-09
**Question**: Is claude-fable-5 ($10/$50 per MTok) worth the money for any extraction metric?
**Worktree**: `~/worktrees/fable-5-extraction/` (btcopilot + fdserver, branch `fable-5-extraction`)
**Total spend**: ≈ $56 (interrupted twice by account credit exhaustion)

## Answer

Yes, for two metrics — via two independent levers:

1. **Fable 5 on extraction (Pass 1+2)**: Events F1 0.592 vs 0.427 (+39%), aggregate
   +0.06. ~$0.83/discussion, 71–179 s/discussion (async-only).
2. **Fable 5 on SARF review (Pass 3 only) — the hybrid**: SARF macro 0.535 vs 0.375
   (+43%) with Events/aggregate unchanged, at **~$0.20/discussion** and ~1 extra
   Fable call per extraction. The cheapest quality lift measured on this pipeline.

Combined (all-Fable): record on every metric — aggregate 0.731, Events 0.617,
SARF macro 0.621 (S 0.688, A 0.574, R 0.452, F 0.770), ~$1.30/discussion.

## Phase 3 — Blockers

| Class | Item |
|---|---|

| gt_noise / metric | `match_events` married person/spouse slot asymmetry — AI picking the other partner can never match; affects ALL models; fix in f1_metrics.py (canonicalize spouse pairs). |
| gt_noise | GT "New Event" placeholders; GT statement-date anchoring vs AI historical anchoring. |
