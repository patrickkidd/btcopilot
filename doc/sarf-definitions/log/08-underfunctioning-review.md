### Key Insights for Underfunctioning

1. **Not inherent incapacity**: The underfunctioner may be far more capable than the pattern allows; revealed when overfunctioner unavailable.

2. **Self-absorption is not selfishness**: Results from having given up too much self-control, not from character flaw.

3. **Symptoms from withdrawal**: Underfunctioner becomes symptomatic through withdrawal, helplessness, self-absorption.

4. **Dramatic improvement possible**: When overfunctioner becomes ill/dies, underfunctioner often improves dramatically.

5. **Extreme dependency**: At extreme, may rely on other to tell them how to think, feel, act.
