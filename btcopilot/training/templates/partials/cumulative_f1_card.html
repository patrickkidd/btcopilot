How is this different from per-statement F1?

Per-statement F1 compares AI vs GT deltas at each individual statement, then averages. Statements with no pair bonds get trivial 1.0 scores, inflating the average.

Cumulative F1 builds the full accumulated PDP from all statements in a discussion, then compares the final AI PDP against the final GT PDP. This better reflects the end-user experience and avoids inflation from empty-vs-empty matches.
